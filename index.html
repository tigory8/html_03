About us

The firm – “Brone-Door” was founded more than 15 years ago. In the beginning, the main activity of the firm was manufacture of metal doors with different decorative coatings and providing services for measurements and installation and warranty service of our products. Additionally we started the production of MDF doors near 5 years ago. When we making our products, we try to take into account all the wishes of our customers regarding: the sizes, materials, colors and the use door’s accessories of the world famous brands Gerda, Mul-T-Lock, Abloy etc. We also offer our clients not only a responsible attitude but also short deadlines for fulfilling orders, high quality products and warranty and pessimistic service. Thanks to hard work and the established dealer network, our products are sold throughout the territory of Ukraine. We always happy for constructive cooperation and your orders.
